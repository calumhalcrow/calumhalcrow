Bio

I was born and brought up in Shetland, Scotland. After finishing school I went to study Mathematics at the University of Edinburgh. After graduating I felt a change of direction was necessary and so moved into web development and programming. I met my now wife during university and we decided to settle down in Malaysia where she's from. We currently live in the country's capital, Kuala Lumpur, and I currently work as a Perl programmer in Cyberjaya, a tech hub located just outside the city.

Skills

Aside from the Pascal I did at school and the small amount of C I did at uni, I cut my teeth as a web developer on PHP. I moved from there onto Perl and have been hacking away with it for the good part of the last six years. Web Development

I feel the essence of my work is to create websites with semantic and standards compliant HTML, clean CSS and unobtrusive JavaScript. I can't help but be a perfectionist in this and often go the extra mile to ensure things are done "right". JQuery is such a handy tool for creating rich interactive web apps; I use it whenever I can. I also have some experience with CMSs like MODx, and with templating engines like Template Toolkit and Django templates. I like GAE as a platform for developing and hosting apps; this site is hosted on it in fact!

Databases
